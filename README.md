# Business Analyst Portfolio — Ainul Fayad

## Short Positioning Statement

I am a recent Business Administration graduate building a Business Analyst / Implementation Analyst portfolio focused on practical business documentation, workflow improvement, requirements, UAT, reporting, and operational problem-solving.

This portfolio uses synthetic case studies to demonstrate how I would approach real business analysis work without claiming real client delivery, real ERP configuration, or production implementation results.

## Portfolio Focus

- Process improvement for operational workflows.
- ERP-style request, approval, handoff, and reporting processes.
- Requirements documentation, business rules, and traceability.
- UAT planning, test cases, and defect tracking.
- SQL analysis and dashboard specification.
- Rollout support, training support, risk tracking, and implementation readiness.

## Featured Project

### ERP Procurement Request to Purchase Order Implementation Case

Synthetic Business Analyst portfolio case study for an ERP-style procurement request-to-purchase-order workflow at a fictional Atlantic Canada marine services company.
